Burger King

Burger King is one of the largest and most well-known fast-food chains in the world, famous for its flame-grilled burgers, especially the Whopper. Founded in 1954 in Miami, Florida, by James McLamore and David Edgerton, Burger King quickly grew to become a major player in the fast-food industry. The chain is known for its slogan "Have it your way," emphasizing customization and choice for customers.
